Data Analytics Virtual Internship

Master the art of turning raw data into powerful business insights. Learn the tools and techniques used by top companies to drive strategy and growth.

What You'll Learn

This program provides a comprehensive, hands-on journey through the entire data analytics lifecycle. You will gain practical experience in:

  • Cleaning, transforming, and preparing complex datasets for analysis.
  • Building beautiful and interactive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau.
  • Writing complex SQL queries to extract and manipulate data from relational databases.
  • Applying statistical methods in Excel to uncover trends and patterns.
  • Developing a strong foundation in data storytelling to present your findings effectively.

Tools You'll Master

Power BI Microsoft Excel SQL Tableau Python (Basics)

Hands-On Projects

You won't just learn theory. You'll build a portfolio of real-world projects, including:

  • An interactive sales dashboard for a global retail company.
  • A customer segmentation analysis to identify high-value clients.
  • A market trend forecast based on historical performance data.
